Accessibility Audits

In Ontario, it is now a legal requirement for all new websites to be accessible to people with disabilities. If you are concerned about whether your website meets the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA) standards, I can help you conduct a thorough review to ensure compliance and optimal usability for all users. Let me work with you to make sure your website is not only compliant, but also fully functional and user-friendly for everyone.
